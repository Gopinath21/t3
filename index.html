About Us

NARAYANA ENGINEERING COLLEGE::NELLORE

Mr P.Narayana an educationalist promoted this institute Narayana Engineering College in the year 1998 .The branch electronics and computers popularly known as ECM was started as a new branch from the very beginning of the college . This course equips students with hardware as well as software knowledge and aims to give them the ability to organize the hardware and software into the best of use. This course is versatile in its contents with the students studying the amalgamation of both the domains of Computers and Electronics. An hands on approach is adopted to improvise the practical methodology of students.

Dr. G.Jagadeeswar Reddy M.Tech.,Ph.D. Principal

Dr. G. Jagadeeswar Reddy is a sincere academician and good Administrator. He Completed his B.Tech from S.V University, M.Tech from Mysore University and Ph.D from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad in Electronics & Communications Engineering. Dr. G Jagadeeswar Reddy attended an IEEE international conference conducted at Penang, Malaysia in Dec 2008, Where his paper has got best paper award and is published in IEEE explorer and in an international journal IJICT

ECM Department

T. Sai Prasad Reddy, Head of the Department

T.Sai prasad Reddy, the Head of ECM Department is a role model to the students. He completed his M.Tech,M.I.S.T.E from Satyabhama University. Under his emminent direction, the students of ECM are progressing towards success.

ELECTRONICS AND COMPUTER ENGINEERING (ECM) branch is a blend of two core fields electronics n computers. this branch was started with an aim to promote the talents of people who are interested into the cores of electronics and computers and need a platform to work on..

The department was established in 1999 with intake of 40 students and was enhanced to 60 later on. Within short period of time with dedicated and sincere efforts of experienced faculty, Dept achieved excellent performance from students. To illustrate a few, Ms. K. Bargavi in 2003, Ms. N. Deepti Lakshmi in 2004, Ms N. Chandana in 2005 & Ms B. Haritha in 2007 have bagged the JNTU gold medals. The students have got prizes in paper presentation held in various institutions. The Dept encourages in developing their personality skills.
